Gaming

Playing video games has always been my favorite hobby and a way to kill time since I was around

From there, my love for video games have grown more and more, as I become fonder of them.
Some other consoles that I've owned include:

  3. PS4
  4. PS4 Slim

  6. PS5
  7. Xbox Series X
  8. Gaming Laptop, the Lenovo Legion Pro 5i

Music

Music... well, who doesn't love listening to music? I personally do! In my free time or even doing
assignments as I currently am typing this, I often listen to music to help myself relax and be more
productive in my work. I like listening to English, Japanese (J-Pop) and Korean (K-Pop) songs, as well
as music like Lo-Fi which helps me feel at ease. Some of my favourite singers and groups include
NewJeans, wave to earth, keshi, One Direction, Charlie Puth, Shawn Mendes, YOASOBI and
Le Sserafim. In the future, I am considering of picking up and learning a music instrument, specifically
a guitar, as I used to like to play the guitar when I was little back then. Or... maybe a piano? Who knows?

Sports

See, I do like to play a few sports, like football, badminton and basketball. But to be honest, I am
not really that athletic... I am more of a sports-watcher kind of guy. I love watching Formula 1 and
seeing fast cars go racing, since my love for cars was influenced by a few of my friends. I sometimes
go karting at a few tracks near KL and Selangor whether alone or with my friends whenever I have
the time and money to go. Scuderia Ferrari and Mercedes AMG Petronas are the teams that I support
in F1. Lewis Hamilton and Charles Leclerc are some of my favourite drivers currently. Other than that,
I love to watch football as well, and would like to go to a stadium in Europe and being able to witness
a match with my own eyes. My first ever match that I watched was in 2013, when I was 9 years old,
which was a friendly between Malaysia against FC Barcelona in Shah Alam Stadium. As for the teams
that I support in football, I go for Manchester United which is a team that my family supports, as well
as FC Barcelona, ever since that match in Shah Alam Stadium, as well as the fact that my favourite
player, Lionel Messi, used to play there.
